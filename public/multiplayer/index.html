Two Player Rules

2 players fight to the death in the Conway Game of Life 1v1 adaptation. Each player can place pixels on their side of their board, and after submission, a simulation will be ran to see who will remain victorious!

Rules:

1. Any live cell with two or three live neighbours survives.

2. Any dead cell with three live neighbours becomes a live cell of the player with the most neighbors for that cell.

3. All other live cells die in the next generation. All other dead cells stay dead.

If a player reaches a cell population count of 0 while the other player still has cells remaining, that player wins. If the number of iterations reaches its end or a stale state occurs, whoever has the most cells at the end is the winner.
